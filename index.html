Designing the Future of Space Debris Cleaning

Vision AI Net

Vision AI Net is our proprietary intelligence engine designed to detect, predict, and assist robotic operations in clearing orbital debris. It leverages a distributed AI system trained on thousands of orbital scenarios to deliver real-time analysis and trajectory predictions with over 95% accuracy.

Our Vision AI is currently integrated into simulated testbeds and is ready for deployment in coordination with autonomous orbital robots.

The Problem

There are over 36,000 pieces of trackable space debris in Earth's orbit, costing $1.5 billion annually in damage and threat to satellites and future missions. With the rise of mega-constellations like Starlink, this issue is escalating rapidly.

Our Solution

DebrisX utilizes AI-enhanced tracking, predictive analytics, and autonomous robots to intercept and neutralize space debris with 40% lower cost and higher efficiency than legacy systems. Our robots mimic the mechanics of ELSA-d and are integrated with a command-control center powered by AWS and NVIDIA Jetson modules.

Minimum Viable Product

Debris Detection System

AI-powered optical tracking system capable of identifying debris as small as 1cm in diameter with 99% accuracy.

Trajectory Prediction

Real-time orbital path calculation and collision risk assessment using our proprietary Vision AI Net.

Autonomous Navigation

Self-guided debris interception system with advanced obstacle avoidance and fuel optimization.
